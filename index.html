Discover the Essence of Wellness

Founded by Janith Buddhika, Madara Spa has been a beacon of tranquility in Madaragama, Koulara, Sri Lanka. Our philosophy blends ancient Ayurvedic traditions with contemporary wellness practices to create a holistic healing experience.

Every detail of our spa has been thoughtfully designed to transport you from the stresses of daily life into a realm of pure relaxation. From the moment you step through our doors, you are enveloped in an atmosphere of calm, luxury, and personalized care.

JB

Janith Buddhika

Founder & Wellness Director

"Creating spaces where healing happens naturally is my life's passion."

Our Mission

To provide transformative wellness experiences that nurture the body, calm the mind, and uplift the spirit through authentic, personalized care.

Our Vision

To be Sri Lanka's most trusted wellness destination, recognized globally for excellence in holistic healing and authentic spa experiences.
